About The Event

Tech Extravaganza 2.0 is a technology exhibition and competition organized by Government Technical High School Kurichy. The event provides students an opportunity to showcase creativity, innovation, engineering skills and technical knowledge.

Events

🏗 Still Model Exhibition

Students can present creative and innovative static models based on science and technology.

🏠 Applied Construction

Showcase construction ideas, designs and practical engineering concepts.

⚡ Electronics Working Model

Demonstrate working electronic projects and innovative circuits.

Rules & Regulations

  • Registration is free.
  • Participants must carry valid school ID cards.
  • Report 30 minutes before the event.
  • Participants are responsible for their models.
  • Maintain discipline inside the campus.
  • Judges' decision will be final.
  • Misconduct may lead to disqualification.
